The project

More than a blog. A scientific portfolio.

ScienceAlchemy was born from an intentional transition: from hospital clinical pharmacy toward clinical research and trials. With over a decade treating complex infectious diseases, the mission is to translate deep pharmacological knowledge into rigorous, useful, uncompromised content.

This is not generic wellness content. It's applied pharmacological analysis grounded in real clinical practice — reviewed with the same rigor used to evaluate a clinical protocol.
